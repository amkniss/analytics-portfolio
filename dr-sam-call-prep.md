## 3. Brand & Business Context

### What Dr. Sam's Does Well
- **Clinical authority:** Dr. Sam Bunting is a real, qualified dermatologist with 10+ years of clinical practice. This is rare in DTC skincare and is their biggest competitive moat.
- **Community:** 22,000-member private Facebook group ("Flawless Tribe") with obsessively engaged users — extremely high-trust environment.
- **Loyalty program:** 150,000+ Smile.io members. 98.71% redemption rate in the first week of a new reward launch. This is exceptional.
- **Subscription model:** Flawless365 program via Recharge — recurring revenue, high LTV customers.
- **Retail wins:** Sephora UK launch sold out hero SKUs within 2 weeks. Space NK partnership launched February 2025. These are signals of real consumer demand.
- **Hero products:** Flawless Brightly Serum (Vitamin C), Flawless Neutralise (Azelaic Acid), SPF 50+ range.

### What's Holding Them Back at Scale
1. The digital experience doesn't match the clinical brand premium.
2. The site is technically underperforming on mobile (45/100 PageSpeed) — the platform where 60%+ of DTC beauty traffic now lands.
3. AI search engines (Google SGE, ChatGPT, Perplexity) are citing the clinical advice but not attributing it to Dr. Sam's products — because the structured data doesn't connect them.
4. The country selector overlay creates a decision gate *before* the page paints — it's the first thing 100% of new visitors hit.

### The Sephora Opportunity Angle
Their Sephora UK sell-out is a *perfect* proof point that demand exists. The question is: **why isn't drsambunting.com capturing the same overflow?** Because when someone googles "Dr Sam azelaic acid" after seeing the Sephora display, the website experience doesn't convert them at the rate it should. This is the exact gap to name.

## 7. Keyword & SEO Intelligence

### High-Opportunity Keywords (Low to Medium Competition)
- **"NAD+ skincare benefits"** — ~1,200/mo, Low competition. Long-form medical guide play.
- **"Azelaic acid vs retinol for acne"** — ~1,600/mo, Medium competition. Blog + Neutralise PDP bridge opportunity.
- **"Non-comedogenic routine for rosacea"** — ~850/mo, Medium competition. Dedicated landing page.
- **"Dermatologist skincare for perimenopause"** — ~500/mo, Low competition. Niche authority play.

### AI Search Gap (GEO)
Currently, when someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity *"what's the best dermatologist-recommended azelaic acid serum?"*, Dr. Sam Bunting appears as a trusted **person** but Dr. Sam's Neutralise does not appear as a **recommended product**. This is a structural schema problem, not a content quality problem. Their content is excellent. The metadata doesn't connect the expertise to the SKU.

### The Cannibalization Risk
Blog posts (e.g., "How to Use Azelaic Acid") and Product Detail Pages are competing for the same keyword cluster. Google is choosing the blog post because it has more inbound links — which means the purchase-intent page (PDP) is being depressed in organic rankings.

## 8. GEO/AI Search Audit

### Where Dr. Sam's Appears in AI Search
- Dr. Sam Bunting herself is cited in AI responses about rosacea, azelaic acid, vitamin C layering, and perimenopause skin
- The Flawless Brightly and Neutralise products are *not reliably* surfaced as product recommendations in AI search engines

### What's Missing
- `MedicalEntity` JSON-LD on Dr. Sam's profile / About page
- `FAQPage` schema on the Expert Advice blog
- `Product` schema with clinical claims properly annotated
- No explicit linking between expert author = product formulator in structured data

### The Fix
- 2–4 hours of schema injection work
- Connects the doctor's authority to the product recommendations
- Positions Dr. Sam's to win AI citations as AI search share grows (currently estimated at 15–25% of beauty discovery queries and accelerating)

## 10. Priority Recommendations (Your 90-Day Plan)

### Phase 1: Stop the Bleeding (Weeks 1–4)
1. **Kill the Country Selector Overlay** — Replace with geo-IP auto-detection + subtle header flag. Est. +15% reduction in new visitor bounce. Estimated recovery: £10k–£20k/month.
2. **Resolve the Checkout Script Conflict on Safari** — Likely a Recharge + Shogun + Klarna JS conflict. Stop losing purchases that are already in-cart.
3. **Implement Sticky Add-to-Cart on Mobile PDPs** — This is a 2-hour Shopify theme edit. Industry benchmark: 8–12% mobile CVR lift.

### Phase 2: Lift the Ceiling (Weeks 5–8)
4. **Optimize Mobile LCP** — Lazy-load hero videos, compress images above fold, audit Cloudflare Rocket Loader interactions with Shogun.
5. **Inject MedicalEntity + FAQPage Schema** — Connect Dr. Sam Bunting's clinical credentials to product pages. Fix keyword cannibalization via canonical tags.
6. **Rewrite Neutralise PDP H2s** — From "The Benefits" to "The Science of Redness Reduction: How Neutralise Works" — language that matches AI query patterns.

### Phase 3: Own the Category (Weeks 9–12)
7. **GEO Content Layer** — Publish 3–4 long-form medical guides targeting AI search queries. Build "Expert Advice" → "Shop the Routine" internal link architecture.
8. **AI Search Schema Audit** — Full product + person schema across all PDPs and blog content.
9. **Intent-Based Content Calendar** — Map the top 20 content ideas to keyword intent and product pages. Build the blog-to-product bridge.
